Brazilian Portuguese audiobook narration. Adult male voice, warm, empathetic, intimate, deep and calm. Slow-to-moderate pace, with natural breathing and thoughtful pauses. The tone should feel like a private conversation, not a commercial voice-over, not theatrical, not motivational, not robotic.

The narrator should sound mature, present and emotionally honest. Use restrained emotion, quiet strength and subtle vulnerability. The strength of the narration should come from silence, pauses and presence, not from dramatic intensity.

Maintain the same voice, rhythm, emotional tone and interpretation throughout the entire book.
